the team

Fellow students, we have been oppressed. Opressed by the very people trying to pull us out of our ignorance. We have been opressed by our college professors, who assign us textbooks as expenisve as their weight in gold, and even moreso by this so-called pearson and other equally tyrannical publishing companies, who markup our textbook inexplicable amounts each new edition. But no more! We the students, in order to make a more balanced budget, do hearby create pynched, a website by the students, for the students, to compare textbook prices through the ages!
